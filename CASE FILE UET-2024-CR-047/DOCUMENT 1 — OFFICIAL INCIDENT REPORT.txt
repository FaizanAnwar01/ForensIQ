INCIDENT REPORT
Case No: UET-2024-CR-047
Reporting Officer: Inspector Naveed Akhtar
Date Filed: 15 November 2024

SUMMARY OF INCIDENT:
On the morning of 15 November 2024, Dr. Imran Siddiqui, supervisor of Research Lab 3B
in Block C, UET Taxila, arrived at 08:10 AM to find the lab door unlocked and two NVIDIA
A100 GPU cards missing from the research workstation designated WS-04. The GPU cards
are university property, each valued at PKR 850,000 (total loss: PKR 1,700,000).

Additionally, an audit triggered by the theft revealed a discrepancy in the lab's
equipment procurement records. A purchase order (PO-2024-309) dated 22 October 2024
was approved for four 2TB external hard drives at PKR 45,000 each (total: PKR 180,000).
University accounts confirmed payment was processed on 25 October 2024. However, only
two hard drives are physically present in the lab. No delivery receipt exists for the
remaining two units.

PHYSICAL EVIDENCE:
- Lab door lock showed no signs of forced entry.
- Internal RFID access log shows lab was accessed at 21:47 on 14 November 2024.
  The access card used was registered to Asad Mehmood (Lab Technician).
- The same RFID log shows the lab was next accessed at 08:10 AM on 15 November by
  Dr. Imran Siddiqui.
- WS-04 side panel was found open. Screwdriver marks present on GPU bracket.
- Security camera in corridor outside Lab 3B malfunctioned between 20:00–23:00
  on 14 November (fault logged by security office at 20:03).

PERSONS OF INTEREST:
1. Asad Mehmood — Lab Technician (card used for 21:47 access)
2. Zara Qureshi — MS Research Student, assigned to WS-04
3. Hamza Tariq — Final Year Project student, regular lab visitor
